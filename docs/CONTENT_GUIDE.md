# Content Sourcing & Sincerity Guide

All entries within the ViceBase CDN repository must adhere to the highest standard of accuracy, sourcing, and legal caution.

---

## 🚫 Legal Requirements

1. **NO COPYRIGHT INFRINGEMENT**: Do not upload copyrighted screenshots, trailer frames, posters, audio clips, or game assets.
2. **USE PUBLIC REFERENCES**: Link directly to official YouTube trailers, public Press Releases, or Rockstar Games Newswire announcements.
3. **ORIGINAL WORKS ONLY**: If visual images are needed, they must be original drawings, UI vectors, or mockups created by the team.

---

## 🔍 Data Integrity Rules

1. **NO SPECULATION**: Do not invent mission names, weapon stats, vehicle speeds, or map boundaries. If a detail is not officially announced or visible in verified material, it must remain omitted.
2. **VERIFIED SOURCES ONLY**:
   - Official trailers released by Rockstar Games.
   - Rockstar Games Newswire posts.
   - Official Take-Two Interactive press releases.
   - Official press coverage (e.g., IGN, GameSpot interviews with developers).
3. **STYLING & METADATA**:
   - All entries must have a status of `"confirmed"` or `"official"` until verified in the final game.
   - `"community"` can be used for community maps and findings once confirmed as community creations.
